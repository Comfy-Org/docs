This guide covers how to use Comfy Desktop after installation.

![Comfy Desktop homepage](/images/desktop/usage/01-homepage.png)

When you open Comfy Desktop you'll see the **Chooser** — a grid of all your ComfyUI installations. Filter chips at the top let you narrow the view to Local, Cloud, or Remote installations. Click any card to launch it, or click **+** to add a new one.
