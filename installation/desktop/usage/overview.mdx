This guide covers how to use Comfy Desktop after installation.

![Comfy Desktop homepage](/images/desktop/usage/01-homepage.png)

After installing and launching Comfy Desktop, you'll see all your ComfyUI instances displayed as cards. From here you can launch any instance, add new ones, manage settings, and more.

This guide is organized into the following sections:

- **[Instance Management](/installation/desktop/usage/instance-management)** — Create, rename, edit, and delete ComfyUI instances.
- **[Managing Installations](/installation/desktop/usage/manage)** — Launch, update, snapshot, and configure your installations through the Manage panel.
- **[Settings](/installation/desktop/usage/settings)** — Global settings — theme, auto-updates, proxy/mirror configuration, and data locations.
- **[Migrate from Legacy](/installation/desktop/usage/migrate)** — Upgrade from Desktop Legacy to Comfy Desktop.
