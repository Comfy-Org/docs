**Stable Audio** is Stability AI's open-source audio generation model family, capable of producing high-quality stereo audio — including music, sound effects, and instruments — from text descriptions. Multiple versions are natively supported in ComfyUI.

**Key features**:

- **Text-to-audio generation** — Turn short descriptions into stereo audio clips
- **Multiple model versions** — Choose between Stable Audio 1.0 for lightweight generation or Stable Audio 3 for advanced quality
- **Category-aware reprompting** (3.0 Medium) — Optional Qwen-powered prompt expansion using presets: Music, Instrument, SFX, One-shot
- **Local inference** — Runs entirely on your machine
- **Stereo output** — Generates full stereo audio, not mono

**Related links**:
- [GitHub: Stability-AI/stable-audio-3](https://github.com/Stability-AI/stable-audio-3)
- [Hugging Face (Comfy-Org/stable-audio-3)](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/stable-audio-3)

**How to use**:
1. **Text prompt** — Enter a description of the audio you want (e.g. "heaven church electronic dance music")

![Stable Audio 3 Medium workflow](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Comfy-Org/workflow_templates/main/templates/audio_stable_audio_3_medium-1.webp)

The **Stable Audio 3 Medium** workflow is a full-featured text-to-audio generation pipeline. You provide a short text idea, optional duration, seed, and category — the workflow expands your prompt using Qwen with a **category-aware reprompt template**, then generates stereo audio via the Stable Audio 3 checkpoint.

**How to use**:
1. **Text idea** — Enter a short description of the sound, music, or effect you want (e.g. "upbeat electronic dance track with heavy bass")
2. **Duration** — Set the desired clip length in seconds (default varies)
3. **Seed** — Control reproducibility by adjusting the seed value
4. **Category** — Choose a reprompt preset: **Music**, **Instrument**, **SFX**, or **One-shot**
5. **Enable reprompt** — Toggle `use_reprompt` on to let Qwen expand your short idea into a detailed prompt before generation
6. Click **Run** (`Ctrl/Cmd + Enter`) to generate. The audio will be saved to `ComfyUI/output/audio/`

## Model download for local users

### Stable Audio 1.0

Place in `ComfyUI/models/checkpoints/`:
- [`stable-audio-open-1.0.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/StabilityAI/stable-audio-open-1.0/resolve/main/stable-audio-open-1.0.safetensors)

Place in `ComfyUI/models/text_encoders/`:
- [`t5-base.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/t5-base/resolve/main/t5-base.safetensors)

### Stable Audio 3

Place in `ComfyUI/models/checkpoints/`:
- [`stable_audio_3_medium.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/stable-audio-3/resolve/main/checkpoints/stable_audio_3_medium.safetensors) (Medium workflow)
- [`stable_audio_3_medium_base.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/stable-audio-3/resolve/main/checkpoints/stable_audio_3_medium_base.safetensors) (Medium Base workflow)

Place in `ComfyUI/models/text_encoders/`:
- [`t5gemma_b_b_ul2.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/stable-audio-3/resolve/main/text_encoders/t5gemma_b_b_ul2.safetensors)
- [`qwen3.5_2b_bf16.safetensors`](https://huggingface.co/Comfy-Org/Qwen3.5/resolve/main/text_encoders/qwen3.5_2b_bf16.safetensors) (only needed for the Medium workflow with Qwen reprompt)

After placing the files, restart ComfyUI and load the workflow.
