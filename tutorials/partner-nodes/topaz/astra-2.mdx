Enhanced creative upscaling for GenAI video, with advanced control over detail and style.

## Model overview

Astra 2 is an advancement in creative diffusion-based upscaling for GenAI video, building on Astra 1 with expanded control over detail generation and stylization. It transforms AI-generated footage by adding new, dynamic detail and texture, with adjustable creativity and sharpness controls and prompt-based guidance for more precise creative direction.

Astra 2 performs best on stylized and GenAI sources, as well as wide shots and large-scale scenes where additional detail can be creatively inferred. It enhances clarity, richness, and visual complexity, and is optimized for expressive, stylized workflows.

It also excels when footage feels overly smooth or “plastic” but needs believable texture; on crowds, landscapes, and other wide shots; on stylized or animation-like clips; and on textiles and complex surfaces where extra richness helps.

In ComfyUI, access the model through the [TopazVideoEnhance](/built-in-nodes/TopazVideoEnhance) partner node.

## Key capabilities

- **Creative transformation of GenAI sources** — Adds new detail and textures, not only sharpening existing pixels.
- **Expanded control** — Adjustable creativity and sharpness, plus prompt-based guidance for precise creative direction.
- **Strong on stylized content, wide shots, and detail-sparse scenes** — Where inferred detail and texture matter most.
