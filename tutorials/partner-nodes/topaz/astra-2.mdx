Enhanced creative upscaling for GenAI video, with advanced control over detail and style.

## What is Astra 2?

**If you're new here:** Astra 2 is a **video enhancement model** focused on **AI-generated and stylized footage**. A basic upscale mostly enlarges the frame and sharpens pixels that already exist. Astra 2 goes further: it can **add believable new detail and texture** where the source is smooth or vague—within limits you set—so the clip looks richer and less “flat,” not only bigger.

**In ComfyUI:** Use it in a graph with the partner node **Topaz Video Enhance**—for example load a video, run it through that node with an Astra-style configuration, then save the output (the example workflow below shows one way to wire this).

## Model overview

Astra 2 builds on Astra 1 with broader control over **how** detail and stylization are generated. It introduces new dynamic detail and texture into AI-generated footage, with adjustable creativity and sharpness and optional prompt-based guidance so you can steer the look.

It performs best on stylized and GenAI sources, wide shots, and large scenes where detail can be inferred creatively—improving clarity, richness, and visual complexity for expressive workflows.

It also tends to help when footage feels overly smooth or “plastic” but needs believable texture; on crowds, landscapes, and other wides; on stylized or animation-like clips; and on textiles and complex surfaces where extra richness matters.

## Key capabilities

- **Creative transformation of GenAI sources** — Adds new detail and textures, not only sharpening existing pixels.
- **Expanded control** — Adjustable creativity and sharpness, plus prompt-based guidance for precise creative direction.
- **Strong on stylized content, wide shots, and detail-sparse scenes** — Where inferred detail and texture matter most.
