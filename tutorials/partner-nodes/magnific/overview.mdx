[Magnific AI](https://magnific.com) is an AI-powered image enhancement platform that offers professional-grade tools for upscaling, relighting, style transfer, and portrait retouching. ComfyUI integrates Magnific through a set of partner nodes, letting you chain these capabilities directly into your workflows — no external app switching required.

<ReqHint/>
<UpdateReminder/>

## What you can do

Magnific partner nodes cover five image enhancement capabilities:

- **Image Relight** — re-light a subject with a text prompt and optional reference image. Control prompt strength, relight mode, background preservation, and shadow generation.
- **Image Style Transfer** — transfer the artistic style of a reference image onto your input. Fine-tune with strength, creativity, reference influence, and style fidelity.
- **Image Upscale Creative** — upscale while adding generative detail. Specify a creative prompt, upscale factor (up to 4×), and creative/fidelity/guidance parameters.
- **Image Upscale Precise** — upscale with maximum fidelity to the original. Supports 2× upscaling with model type selection, noise and blur reduction, and detail enhancement.
- **Skin Enhancer** — smooth and refine skin naturally. Control smoothing and denoise strength, and choose between creative and natural enhancement modes.

## Available workflows
