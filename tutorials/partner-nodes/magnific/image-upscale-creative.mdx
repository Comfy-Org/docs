### Steps to run

1. In **Load Image**, load the image you want to upscale
2. In **Magnific Image Upscale Creative**, configure the **prompt** describing the content of the image (helps guide detail generation)
3. Select the **upscale_factor** (up to 16Ă—)
4. Choose **creativity** level to control how much new detail is hallucinated
5. Adjust **resemblance**, **hdr**, **sharpen**, and **noise** to fine-tune the output
6. Under **preset** (advanced), choose a preset for common scenarios (`automatic` recommended)
7. Optionally enable **tune_color** for color correction
8. Click **Run** or press `Ctrl(cmd) + Enter`
