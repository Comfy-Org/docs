Seedance 2.0 is ByteDance's next-generation multimodal video generation model, now available inside ComfyUI. It accepts text, images, video, and audio as unified inputs and produces high-quality video with synced audio, consistent characters, and cinematic camera motion in a single pass.

## Key capabilities

- **Native multimodal input** — Text, images, video, and audio can all be used as conditioning. Combine up to 9 reference images, 3 reference videos, and 3 reference audio clips, and the model keeps characters, objects, style, and sound coherent across the output
- **Synchronized audio generation** — Video and audio are generated jointly, with visuals aligned to dialogue, music, and sound effects at a phoneme level, including multiple languages and dialects
- **Cinematic camera control** — Replicate camera movement, editing rhythm, and action choreography from a reference clip to get stable long takes, dolly/pan/zoom moves, and complex shot compositions
- **Character and scene consistency** — Facial features, clothing, materials, and lighting remain stable across the entire video, making it well suited for narrative scenes and branded content
- **Precise video editing** — Swap subjects, add/remove/modify objects, and inpaint regions of existing footage without regenerating from scratch
- **Flexible video extension** — Extend clips forward, generate preceding scenes, or interpolate between two separate clips to turn short footage into a complete story

With these capabilities, Seedance 2.0 fits a wide range of use cases including advertising, short-form drama, film previsualization, product showcases, virtual character content, and assets for games and animation.

## Available workflows

- **Text to video** — Generate video from a text prompt
- **Reference to video** — Use reference images, videos, or audio to guide generation
- **First-last-frame to video** — Provide a starting and ending frame to generate the video between them
- **Real Person** — Generate videos featuring real people with identity consistency after a one-time verification. See [Seedance 2.0 Real Person](/tutorials/partner-nodes/bytedance/seedance-2-0-real-human) for details.
