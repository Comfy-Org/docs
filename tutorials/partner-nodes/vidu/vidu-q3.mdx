## Text to Video (Q3)

The **Vidu3TextToVideoNode** is Vidu's highest quality text-to-video node. It excels at generating complex cinematic scenes with multi-shot descriptions, including camera movements, transitions, and audio directions.

**Parameters:**
- **model**: Uses `viduq3-pro`
- **aspect_ratio**: `16:9`, `9:16`, or other supported ratios
- **resolution**: Defaults to `720p`
- **duration**: Video length in seconds (up to 10; default 6)
- **generate_audio**: Boolean — optionally generate audio for the video
- **prompt**: Multi-shot descriptions with cinematography details
- **seed**: Random seed for reproducibility

### Cinematic Prompting

Q3 supports advanced multi-shot prompting. You can describe each shot with timing, camera movement, lighting, color grading, and even audio directions. For example, a chase scene prompt can specify:

- **Shot 1 (0-3s)**: Low-angle tracking shot, camera movement, character actions, dialogue, sound effects
- **Shot 2 (3-6s)**: Wide exterior shot, high angle, slow-motion effects, environmental details
- **Color Grading**: Specify color palette, contrast, and lighting style
- **Audio**: Describe sound design, music, and ambient effects

## Image to Video (Q3)

The **Vidu3ImageToVideoNode** animates a still image with Vidu's latest generation technology. Connect a LoadImage node to the `image` input, write your prompt describing the motion, and run the workflow.

**Parameters:**
- Same as Q3 Text to Video, plus an **image** input
- Multi-shot cinematic prompts work here too
- Audio generation is supported

### Example Workflow

A typical Q3 Image-to-Video workflow:

1. **LoadImage** — Load your starting image
2. **Vidu3ImageToVideoNode** — Connect the image, write a multi-shot prompt describing scene motion, transitions, and audio
3. **SaveVideo** — Save or preview the generated video
