## About FLUX.2 [klein]

FLUX.2 [Klein] is the fastest model in the Flux family, unifying text-to-image and image editing in one compact architecture. - Two 4B types: Base (undistilled) for maximum flexibility and fine-tuning; Distilled (4-step) for speed-first deployments
