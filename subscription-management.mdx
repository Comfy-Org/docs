## How to subscribe

To subscribe to Comfy Cloud, visit and log in to [https://cloud.comfy.org/](https://cloud.comfy.org/). Subscriptions are specifically for Comfy Cloud services.

We use Stripe to manage all subscriptions.

## Subscription renewal

Your subscription renews automatically on the same day and time each month as when you first subscribed.

All Comfy Cloud subscription plans automatically renew unless you set your plan to cancel.

## How to cancel subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Visit and log in to [cloud.comfy.org](https://cloud.comfy.org)
2. Navigate to the Settings menu
3. Go to **Plan & Credits**
4. Click **Manage subscription** to access Stripe and manage your subscription
