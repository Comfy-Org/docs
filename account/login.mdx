## Logging in

To access your ComfyUI account:

1. Navigate to the [ComfyUI login page](https://www.comfy.org/login)
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click the **Log in** button

## Stay logged in

To remain logged in across browser sessions:
- Check the **Remember me** option before logging in
- This will keep you logged in for 30 days on the current device

<Warning>
Only use the "Remember me" option on personal devices. Avoid using it on shared or public computers.
</Warning>

## Forgot your password

If you can't remember your password:

1. Click the **Forgot password?** link on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for password reset instructions
4. Click the reset link in the email
5. Create a new password
6. Log in with your new password

## Two-factor authentication

If you have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled:

1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. You will be prompted for a verification code
3. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
4. Click **Verify** to complete login

## Troubleshooting login issues

If you're having trouble logging in:

- Verify your email address is correct
- Check that Caps Lock is not enabled when entering your password
- Clear your browser cookies and cache
- Try using a different browser or incognito mode
- Ensure your account email has been verified
- Contact [support](/support/contact-support) if issues persist

## Security tips

To keep your account secure:
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared devices
- Regularly update your password
