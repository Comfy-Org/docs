## Prerequisites

Before creating an account, ensure you have:
- A valid email address
- A secure password (minimum 8 characters recommended)

## Creating your account

1. Navigate to the [ComfyUI registration page](https://www.comfy.org/signup)
2. Enter your email address in the email field
3. Create a strong password that meets the security requirements
4. Confirm your password by entering it again
5. Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
6. Click the **Create account** button

## Email verification

After submitting your registration:

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message from ComfyUI
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. You will be redirected to the ComfyUI platform with your account activated

<Note>
If you don't receive the verification email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or request a new verification email.
</Note>

## Next steps

Once your account is created and verified:
- [Log in to your account](/account/login)
- Set up your profile preferences
- Start using ComfyUI features
- Explore tutorials and documentation

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during account creation:
- Ensure your email address is valid and not already registered
- Check that your password meets the minimum requirements
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Contact [support](/support/contact-support) if problems persist
