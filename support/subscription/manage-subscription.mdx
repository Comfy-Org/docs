## How to subscribe

To subscribe to Comfy Cloud:

1. Visit and log in to [cloud.comfy.org](https://cloud.comfy.org/)
2. Select your desired subscription plan
3. Complete the payment process

All subscriptions are managed through Stripe and are specifically for Comfy Cloud services.

## Subscription renewal

Your subscription automatically renews on the same day and time each month as when you first subscribed.

All Comfy Cloud subscription plans automatically renew unless you set your plan to cancel.

## Cancel subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Visit and log in to [cloud.comfy.org](https://cloud.comfy.org/)
2. Navigate to the settings menu
3. Go to **Plan & Credits**
4. Click **Manage subscription** to access Stripe's subscription management portal
5. Follow the prompts to cancel your subscription

Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
