## How to subscribe

You can browse and use Comfy Cloud normally without a subscription, but you need to subscribe to run workflows.

To subscribe to Comfy Cloud, visit and log in to [cloud.comfy.org](https://cloud.comfy.org/).

For detailed information about Comfy Cloud subscription plans and pricing, visit [comfy.org/cloud/pricing](https://www.comfy.org/cloud/pricing).

Comfy Cloud subscriptions are managed through Stripe, our payment processor.

## Using a coupon

If you have a coupon code, you can apply it during the subscription process:

1. Visit [cloud.comfy.org](https://cloud.comfy.org/) and log in to your account.
2. Select your desired subscription plan.
3. During checkout, look for the "Add promotion code" or "Apply coupon" field.
4. Enter your coupon code and click "Apply".
5. The discount will be applied to your subscription price.
6. Complete the payment process to activate your subscription.

Coupon codes may have specific terms and conditions, such as expiration dates or usage limits. Make sure to review the details of your coupon before applying it.

## Subscription renewal

Your subscription renews automatically on the same day and time each month as when you first subscribed.

All Comfy Cloud subscription plans automatically renew unless you set your plan to cancel.
